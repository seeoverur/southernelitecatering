About Southern Elite Catering

Flexible Charleston catering for every type of event and group.

Our Mission

Southern Elite Catering was created with one simple goal in mind: to make professional Charleston catering accessible to all types of groups and events across the Lowcountry. From small gatherings to large celebrations, our mission is to provide reliable food and drink catering that helps hosts focus on their guests rather than the logistics of the event.

Charleston is known for its hospitality, food culture, and social events, and we believe catering should reflect that same spirit. Our team works to deliver meals, service, and presentation that fit the tone of each event—whether it is formal, relaxed, corporate, or celebratory.

Instead of focusing on just one niche, Southern Elite Catering is designed to support a wide range of gatherings. Different groups have different needs, and our approach is to provide flexible catering options that can adapt to those needs while maintaining consistent quality and service.

Catering for Many Types of Groups

Our services are designed for individuals, businesses, and organizations throughout Charleston, South Carolina. Some events require quick and efficient food service, while others benefit from a more curated dining experience. Southern Elite Catering works with a variety of clients including event planners, corporate offices, families hosting private celebrations, and organizations coordinating community events.

By offering multiple catering styles—from buffet service to appetizer displays and beverage support—we aim to provide a practical solution for different guest counts, budgets, and venues. The focus is always on making sure guests enjoy the food while the host enjoys the event.

Events We Commonly Cater
  • Weddings and rehearsal dinners
  • Corporate events and office gatherings
  • Private parties and milestone celebrations
  • Community and nonprofit events
  • Holiday parties and seasonal events
  • School and university functions

Serving Charleston and the Lowcountry

Southern Elite Catering proudly provides catering services throughout the Charleston area including Downtown Charleston, Mount Pleasant, West Ashley, James Island, North Charleston, Daniel Island, and surrounding Lowcountry communities. Our goal is to help make events easier to host by delivering dependable catering service wherever people gather.

Whether the event is a professional gathering, a celebration with friends, or a large community event, our team works to bring great food and smooth service to every table.
