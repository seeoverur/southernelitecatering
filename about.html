Our Story

Designed for events that need more than just food delivery.

Southern Elite Catering was created with a simple vision: offer Charleston clients a catering company that feels both elevated and approachable. In a city known for hospitality, entertaining, and memorable gatherings, catering should support the full atmosphere of the event instead of feeling like an afterthought.

That means paying attention to timing, setup, flow, presentation, and the details guests notice. Whether the event is a wedding reception, a corporate lunch, a brunch celebration, or a private party, the goal is to provide food and drink service that fits the moment and makes hosting easier.
