Catering Options

Choose the level of service that fits your event, your guest list, and the kind of experience you want to create.

On-Site Service

Full-Service Catering

Ideal for weddings, private parties, and business functions where you want a more complete setup. This option can include staffed service, buffet or plated presentation, beverage support, and coordination that helps the event flow naturally from start to finish.

Full-service catering is the right fit when hospitality is part of the experience and you want guests to feel genuinely hosted, not simply fed.

Drop-Off Catering

Simple, Convenient, Ready to Serve

For office lunches, casual celebrations, team gatherings, and daytime events, our drop-off catering option keeps things easy. We prepare the food, package it neatly, and deliver it ready for your guests.

This is a great choice when you want high-quality catering without the need for a full on-site service team.

Custom Menus

Built Around Your Event

Not every event fits a preset package. We also offer custom catering planning for hosts who need a menu or service approach tailored to the occasion, guest preferences, event timing, or venue setup.

Whether you are planning something elegant, casual, social, or business-focused, Southern Elite Catering can help shape a service plan that feels appropriate and intentional.
